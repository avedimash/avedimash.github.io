![Population Health Research Center](/images/hero.jpg)

The Population Health Research Center studies the patterns and drivers of
disease across populations, with a focus on non-communicable diseases and
cardiovascular health in Kazakhstan and the wider Central Asian region.

Our work combines epidemiology, biostatistics, and the Global Burden of
Disease framework to measure how disease burden is changing over time — and
to identify where prevention and health policy can do the most good.

## Dr. Kairat Davletov

![Dr. Kairat Davletov](/images/kairat-davletov.jpg)

Dr. Kairat Davletov leads the Population Health Research Center at
Asfendiyarov Kazakh National Medical University in Almaty. His research
focuses on cardiology, the epidemiology and prevention of non-communicable
diseases, and population health.
