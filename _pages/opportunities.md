We're always looking for curious, motivated people to join our work on
population health and non-communicable disease in Kazakhstan and Central Asia.
We currently have two openings.

## Research Assistant (Volunteer)

We welcome highly motivated volunteers interested in epidemiology,
biostatistics, and public health research. This is a chance to contribute to
real studies, learn data-analysis and research methods hands-on, and
co-author publications.

**You might be a good fit if you:**
- Are an undergraduate or recent graduate in medicine, public health, biology, statistics, or a related field
- Are comfortable with careful, detail-oriented work
- Can commit a regular number of hours per week

## PhD Students

We are recruiting PhD candidates to develop independent research within the
Center's themes — cardiovascular epidemiology, burden of disease, and
prevention. PhD researchers work closely with the team and lead their own
projects toward publication.

**We're looking for candidates who:**
- Hold (or are completing) a relevant master's-level degree
- Have a clear research interest aligned with the Center's work
- Are motivated to publish and present internationally
