The Center's research examines the burden, distribution, and prevention of
non-communicable diseases — with particular emphasis on cardiovascular health
in Kazakhstan and Central Asia.

## Research Themes

- **Cardiovascular epidemiology** — risk factors, prevalence, and outcomes
- **Burden of disease** — Global Burden of Disease analyses for the region
- **Prevention & health policy** — translating evidence into public health action
